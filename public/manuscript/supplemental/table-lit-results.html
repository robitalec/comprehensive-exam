For empirical tests, species and taxonomic classes were recorded. Article information also includes year of publication, journal, authors and title. Literature was obtained using a search for 'producer' and 'scrounger' in the titles, abstracts and keywords of articles in the databases SCOPUS, Web of Science and Biological Abstracts. Literature was filtered to remove duplicates using article's DOI (Script 1).
